About Us

This website is a project and was made for educational purposes.
It is our senior project creation and continous development for our CS 4360 class at Metropolitan State University of Denver. The idea for this project was to create a website that essentially combines the functionalities of both a bookstore and library (in terms of both renting and purchasing books). This project was made possible by Antonio Lopez, Huy Bui, Noemi Marquez, and Jesus Castaneda Magallanes.

Thank you!
-From the OneStop Books Family
